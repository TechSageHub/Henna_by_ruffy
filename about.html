The Story

What started as a passion for henna has grown into a journey of creativity, beauty, and entrepreneurship

Henna_by_ruffy began with a simple love for creating art on skin and is now evolving into a full beauty brand—offering premium services, unique experiences, and training for aspiring artists.

Our Vision

Creating beauty, building a brand, inspiring others.

The Journey

Building trust through henna artistry.

The brand was born from a love for intricate henna work, using detail, patience, and design to create memorable client experiences. As demand grew, so did the vision for something bigger than a single service.

The Expansion

Becoming a modern beauty studio.

Henna_by_ruffy is evolving to offer manicure and pedicure services, facials, lash extensions, massage therapy, and nail tech services while preserving the artistic identity that made the brand stand out.

The Vision

Creating beauty experiences and future professionals.

Beyond serving clients, the long-term goal is to develop a training and mentorship platform where beauty lovers can learn practical skills, refine their craft, and grow alongside the brand.

Brand Promise

Elegant service, genuine care, and creative confidence.

Every new offering is guided by quality, professionalism, and a desire to make clients feel seen, confident, and beautifully cared for.

Client-first experience

Warm, polished service from booking to final reveal.

Creative identity

Henna remains a signature foundation while the brand grows into new beauty spaces.

Future-focused learning

Education, workshops, and mentorship are central to the next stage of growth.
