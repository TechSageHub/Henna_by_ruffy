Henna Booking Policy & Client Guidelines

Please read these policies carefully before booking. Your comfort, safety, and satisfaction are my top priority.

01

Booking & Payment

A non-refundable deposit is required to secure all appointments. Full payment must be completed on or before the day of your appointment.

02

Cancellation

Reschedule with 24–48 hours notice. Same-day cancellations will result in loss of deposit.

03

Late Arrivals

10–15 minute grace period. Arrivals beyond this time may lead to cancellation or reduced design time.

04

Skin Preparation

Arrive with clean, product-free skin. Exfoliate 24h prior. Avoid waxing/shaving the area right before.

05

Design Policy

Agree on designs before the session. Major changes during the session may attract additional charges.

06

Aftercare Responsibility

We provide instructions, but you are responsible for following them for a rich, long-lasting stain.

07

Safety & Allergy Disclaimer

Henna is generally safe, but reactions can occur. Disclose all relevant conditions beforehand.

08

Patch Test Policy

Highly recommended for first-timers or sensitive skin. Request 24h before your appointment.

09

Refusal of Service

Service may be refused if skin is unsuitable (irritation/infection) or policies are not respected.
